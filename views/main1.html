Welcome !
"Food is our common ground, a universal experience."



Rendezvous is a charming Indian restaurant located in the heart of Amritsar. Here, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey through the rich and diverse flavors of India. Our menu features an array of authentic dishes that are carefully crafted using the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Whether you're in the mood for a spicy vindaloo, a creamy korma, or a flavorful biryani, we have something to satisfy every taste bud. At Rendezvous, we aim to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where guests can relax and enjoy the true essence of Indian cuisine. From the colorful décor to the welcoming staff, every detail has been carefully considered to provide an unforgettable dining experience. So why not come and join us at Rendezvous today? Let us transport you to the vibrant streets of India with every bite.We look forward to serving you soon.
