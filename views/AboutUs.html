Our Story
Rendezvous was founded in 2001 by the Sankalp Group of Restaurants. Located in the heart of Punjab which is Amritsar, Rendevouz promises to transport you to a world of taste and aroma. Our warm and inviting atmosphere is the perfect setting for savoring the authentic and delicious dishes of Indian Cuisine, whether you're dining with family and friends, or looking for a romantic night out.




Meet Our Team


Our skilled chefs use traditional cooking techniques and modern flavor profiles to create dishes that are both delicious and innovative. We offer a range of appetizers, entrees, and desserts, all made with the utmost care and attention to detail. At Rendevouz, we believe that dining is an experience, which is why our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always on hand to help make your visit a memorable one. Whether you need recommendations on what to order, or have any special dietary requirements, we are always happy to assist you.
